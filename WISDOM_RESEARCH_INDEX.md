## Key Findings Summary

### Core Insight
All eight wisdom traditions teach the same fundamental truth:
- **Energy is precious**
- **It can be wasted or refined**
- **The critical moment is the threshold (the pause)**
- **Transmutation (not suppression) is the practice**
- **The purpose is virtue/wisdom/awakening, not perfection**

### Validation of Manforge's Framing
- "The energy is the ally" ✓ (all 8 traditions honor vital energy)
- "The compulsion is the enemy" ✓ (all distinguish raw energy from habitual unconscious loop)
- "The man is never the enemy" ✓ (no shame/punishment; all teach awakening to potential)
- "The pause is where mastery begins" ✓ (all converge on threshold moment)
- "Transmutation, not suppression" ✓ (all teach redirection, not destruction)

### Immediate Applications
1. **3–5 new Studies** drawing on traditions (Stoicism, Buddhism, Sufism, Daoism are strongest fits)
2. **1–2 new Rites** (cross-tradition or deep-dive)
3. **Enhanced Codex** with lineage backgrounds and deep-dive links
4. **New Principles** grounded in traditions
